The Story

We went to our favorite spot at the end of Embarcadero, where they serve clam chowder in a bread bowl, and an entertainer plays music and sings along while playing the drums. This is the place Michael planned on asking, but the atmosphere wasn't right.

The Proposal - 4/25/2015

We dropped off a piece of street art back at the car, and as we crossed the street from the park, Clarise says, "We should get married here Michael." Michael pulls the ring out all sly, and says, "I don't know about getting married, but how about engaged?"

Cable Cars

After we were engaged, we took a cable car across SF to the financial district. Michael thought it was a round trip around San Francisco...

Lost in the City...

But it ended up not being so, we had our fun roaming around the city, coming across an 8 story mall, but then it was time to head home. We walked uphill a few blocks, but we were farther than we thought, so the next cable car that passed by we hopped on and took a complimentary ride back to where we started.
